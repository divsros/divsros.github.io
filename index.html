Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MapleMate

Is MapleMate safe to use?
Yes, MapleMate uses no memory editing or cheats.
It operates using special movement algorithms that mimic human patterns with extreme randomness, making it completely safe for your account.
For maximum safety on the official servers, we recommend using a Raspberry Pi device for inputs.
Of course, we recommend semi-monitoring the bot as people can always pass by, and in some cases, 10 seconds of you not replying can be enough to get banned.
What servers does MapleMate support?
MapleMate works on ALL MapleStory servers including KMS, GMS, SEA, MapleStory N, MapleStory Worlds, MapleLand, Artale, and any low or high version server.
What are the 3 bot types included?
The package includes:
Auto Bot farming bot
Script Mode with custom routines
Dynamic Farmer with AI-powered features for active monsters detection.
How does the Telegram integration work?
You can receive alerts for events such as:
Player entering the map
Player is talking to you

You can also take screenshots, record videos, and control the bot remotely through Telegram commands.
What languages are supported?
The interface supports English, Korean, and Chinese. Additional languages can be added upon request.
