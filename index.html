Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MapleMate

Is MapleMate safe to use?
Yes, MapleMate uses no memory editing or cheats. It operates through safe automation methods that mimic real player behavior, making it completely safe for your account.
What servers does MapleMate support?
MapleMate works on ALL MapleStory servers including KMS, GMS, SEA, MapleStory N, MapleStory Worlds, MapleLand, Artale, and any low or high version server.
What are the 3 bot types included?
The package includes: 1) Auto Mode farming bot, 2) Script Mode with custom routines, and 3) Dynamic Farmer with AI-powered features for advanced automation.
How does the Telegram integration work?
You can receive alerts for elite bosses, player detection, white room detection, and lie detector alerts. You can also take screenshots, record videos, and control the bot remotely through Telegram commands.
What languages are supported?
The interface supports English, Korean, and Chinese. Additional languages can be added upon request.
How do I get started?
Contact us through Discord (AutoCuber1) or join our Discord server (discord.gg/HjnRpe3TzF) for pricing and setup assistance. We provide full support to get you started.
