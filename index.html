Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MapleMate

Is MapleMate safe to use?
Yes, MapleMate uses no memory editing or cheats. It operates using special movement algorithms that mimic human patterns with extreme randomness, making it completely safe for your account. Optional: for extra safety on official servers, you can use a Raspberry Pi device for inputs (not required). Of course, we recommend semi-monitoring the bot as people can always pass by.
What servers does MapleMate support?
MapleMate works on ALL MapleStory servers including KMS, GMS, SEA, MapleStory N, MapleStory Worlds, MapleLand, Artale, and any low or high version server.
What are the 4 bot types included?
  • •Auto Bot — Pre-made routine farming
  • •Dynamic Farmer — AI-powered active mob detection
  • •Recorder Mode — Record, follow, auto-correct + randomness
  • •Script Mode — Full control with custom scripts
How does the Telegram integration work?
Receive alerts for: player entering map, player talking to you, white room detection, lie detector, and more. You can also take screenshots, record videos, and control the bot remotely through Telegram commands.
What languages are supported?
The interface supports English, Korean, and Chinese. Additional languages can be added upon request.
How do I get started?
Contact us through Discord (AutoCuber1) or join our Discord server (discord.gg/HjnRpe3TzF). We provide full support to get you started.
