Is MapleMate safe to use?
Yes, MapleMate uses no memory editing or cheats.
It operates using special movement algorithms that mimic human patterns with extreme randomness, making it completely safe for your account.
For maximum safety on the official servers, we recommend using a Raspberry Pi device for inputs.
Of course, we recommend semi-monitoring the bot as people can always pass by, and in some cases, 10 seconds of you not replying can be enough to get banned.

MapleMate works on ALL MapleStory servers including KMS, GMS, SEA, MapleStory N, MapleStory Worlds, MapleLand, Artale, and any low or high version server.
